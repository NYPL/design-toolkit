Alert banner

Requires a content-wrapper for proper margin and padding.

The New York Public Library will be closed on Monday, October 10 in observance of Columbus Day.

<div class="alert">
  <div class="content-wrapper">
    The New York Public Library will be closed on Monday, October 10
    in observance of Columbus Day.
  </div>
</div>

Location card

Note the special classes for each property.

First Floor, Room 100

Phone: (212) 642-0139 | Fax: (212) 930-0838

Fully accessible to wheelchairs


#location-info {
  @include location-info;
}
    

<div id="location-info">
  <p class="room">First Floor, Room 100</p>
  <p class="contact">Phone: (212) 642-0139 | Fax: (212) 930-0838</p>
  <p class="access">Fully accessible to wheelchairs</p>
</div>
